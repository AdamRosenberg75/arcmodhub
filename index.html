The African Research Council has developed the Moderator Hub with a specific focus on addressing challenges within the market research industry.

Moderator Hub Services

Moderator Registration

Moderators register online and provide a detailed biography, skillset list and areas of proficiency.  

Moderator Support

In addition to providing access to a larger pool of potential clientelle, we take care of your scheduling, reminders, invoicing and training opportunities.

Moderator Bookings

Clients and field managers access the booking portal, define their moderating requirements and timeframes, and book from the pool of moderators best-suited to the project.

Feedback & Training

Feedback and ratings on the portal from clients and field managers ensure that moderators are identified for the ARC Upskill Program thereby allowing us to provide an ongoing improvement to moderation services.
